"Citystate Tower Hotel"

Citystate Tower Hotel is at the heart of culturally rich and historic district of Ermita in Manila, Philippines. Vibrant night and day, the district is bustling with activity from nearby business, commercial establishments, entertainment and recreation centers it hosts.With close proximity to embassies, famous landmarks, parks and sites of interest, Citystate Tower Hotel boasts of well-appointed rooms, good food, spacious banquet halls, and sunshine-friendly staff.But more than being an impressive-looking glass and concrete structure in Ermita, it has been, throughout the years, a party place, a business and convention center, a destination where to enjoy sumptuous meals, friendship and family. Your home away from home where your pleasure is our business.
