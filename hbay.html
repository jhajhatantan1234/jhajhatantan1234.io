"Bayview Park Hotel Manila in Manila, Philippines"

1118 Roxas Boulevard corner United Nations Avenue, Manila, 1000, Philippines Located along the famous Roxas Boulevard, Bayview Park Hotel Manila stands proud and strong. For 75 years, it is the most sought-after destination for business and leisure travelers. The hotel provides unparalleled service and exclusive privileges for discriminating guests at affordable rates. Bayview Park Hotel Manila is a walk away from Intramuros and situated opposite the landmark U.S. Embassy building. Being in the historic heart of the city, guests can enjoy the spectacular views of Manila Bay sunsets and nightlife. We guarantee a comfortable and convenient ambiance in this classy and elegant hotel in Manila.
