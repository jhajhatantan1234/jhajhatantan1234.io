"THE PICASSO BOTIQUE SERVICED RESIDENCES"

A masterpiece in its own right, The Picasso is a multi-tiered gem that will enchant its guests with exquisite levels of creativity: from its visionary structure to its imaginative accommodations. Exploring the residences is an immersion in artistic inspiration. The boutique hotel is guaranteed to seduce your senses: experience it once and you will surely dream of repeating your stay at The Picasso. Reinventing the old to experience the new: this is the concept behind The Picasso. The residences are lodged in an old building, but its cutting-edge design rises above the bustling street life it is surrounded with.
