"CASA BOCOBO HOTEL"

Located amid the charming neighborhood of Ermita in Manila, Philippines, Casa Bocobo Hotel welcomes tourists to an accommodation that blends modern comforts with the elegance of the Spanish Colonial Era. The simple luxuries our hotel offers come at an affordable price, and different travelers can find a home in the many conveniences we offer. Draped in warm colors and soft lights, each room provides the comfort you need on a holiday. These include air-conditioning, Wi-Fi Internet access, LCD cable TV, orthopedic bed, and private toilet and shower. To deliver utmost ease, our competent staff renders room service and dishes out scrumptious meals from the restaurant.
